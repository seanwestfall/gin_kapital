Our Story

BTC Kapital is an experimental crowd-funding platform much like other popular crowd-funding sites Kickstarter, Indiegogo, and Crowdrise. Though, instead of traditional methods we use cryptocurrencies to handle all our financial transactions, and smart contracts to handle their operations. For a more detailed explanation of how this platform works, visit our, how it works page.

Bitcoin, and other crypto
